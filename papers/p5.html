Soil structure determines erosion mechanisms: revealing differences in slope erosion and hydraulics of spoil heaps composed of sandy soil and red soil

中文题目：土体结构决定侵蚀机理：揭示砂土与红土弃渣堆的边坡侵蚀及水力学特性差异

关键结果

  • 明确风积砂土的总侵蚀量是红土的 1.5–9.5 倍，其内在可蚀性显著更高。
  • 揭示两种土体的差异化侵蚀阶段特征：红土呈现先快速侵蚀、后稳定调整的阶段特征，砂土侵蚀规律与之存在显著差异。
  • 指出砾石覆盖对两种土体的作用不同：对红土保护效应更稳定，对砂土则呈现更复杂且可能不利的响应。

Abstract

Spoil heaps are prone to severe erosion, yet the role of soil type in modulating their erosional response to hydro-climatic variables is not fully understood. This study comparatively investigated the erosion dynamics of Aeolian sandy soil and red soil via laboratory flume experiments under two rainfall intensities and five gravel coverages.

Aeolian sandy soil exhibited significantly higher total erosion than red soil, highlighting its greater intrinsic erodibility. Increased rainfall intensity generally exacerbated erosion for both soils. Gravel content effects were complex: inducing unstable, sometimes detrimental, responses in Aeolian sandy soil, while offering more predictable protection for red soil, especially under high rainfall intensity.

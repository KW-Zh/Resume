The stress-strain relationship of pebble concrete mixed with recycled brick aggregate under uniaxial compression

中文题目：单轴受压下卵石-再生砖骨料混凝土的应力-应变关系研究

关键结果

  • 明确再生砖骨料替代率 25% 时，混凝土抗压、抗拉强度高于 0% 替代率组，50% 替代率组强度最低。
  • 5–31.5 mm 骨料级配的混凝土抗压强度较 5–16 mm 级配提升约 10%，抗拉强度最高提升 32.28%。
  • 提出并验证了卵石—再生砖骨料混凝土的应力—应变本构关系模型。

Abstract

Demolition activities in the Yellow River Basin have produced substantial waste bricks, accompanied by abundant local pebble. The recycled brick coarse aggregate can be used together with pebbles as aggregate to produce pebble concrete mixed with recycled brick aggregate. However, the mechanical properties of this material under varying strength, aggregate gradations, and substitution rate remain insufficiently understood.

The results indicate that failure mode, elastic modulus, peak stress and peak strain are closely related to substitution rate and aggregate gradation. The study further proposes and validates the stress-strain relationship model for this material system.
